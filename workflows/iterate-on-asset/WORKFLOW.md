# Iterate On Asset

## Goal

Improve one asset through a small loop of drafting, trying, and revising.

## Uses

- Persona: `personas/curious-builder/PERSONA.md`
- Skill: `skills/repo-exploration/SKILL.md`

## Steps

1. Pick one asset to improve.
2. Read the nearest template or existing example.
3. Make one small draft rather than a full system.
4. Try the draft in a practice area.
5. Capture one example and one eval note.
6. Revise only the parts that caused friction.

## Output

An updated asset plus a small trail of evidence showing how it was tested.
